About Us

Access E-Talk Plus Inc. (AETPI) is a proud subsidiary of i-Cube, an education group in Japan with over 50 years of history. i-Cube operates 59 cram schools and supports more than 6,000 students, building a strong foundation in quality education.

For over 17 years, with ESL academic professional, AETPI has been delivering high-quality ESL services, primarily to Japanese learners and students from across Asia. We are widely recognized in Japan for developing innovative ways of teaching English online, creating meaningful learning experiences for both students and teachers.

We believe that great learning begins with great teachers. At AETPI, we are committed to building a community of dedicated ESL educators who inspire confident English communicators. Together, we aim to make English accessible, enjoyable, and transformative for learners of all ages.

Why Choose Us

Team Collaboration

Our trainers and head teachers are here to support you every step of the way. We encourage open communication, shared learning, and collaboration among our ESL teaching team. By providing guidance, feedback, and mentorship, we help you grow your teaching skills, exchange ideas, and succeed in delivering high-quality lessons to your students.

Our company fosters a friendly and supportive environment for our ESL teachers. We value every teacher, promote positivity, and encourage mutual respect in all interactions.
